Agriculture

Agriculture is a significant sector in the Philippines, employing a large portion of the population. Major crops include rice, corn, coconut, and sugarcane. The country is also known for its tropical fruits like mangoes and bananas.

Services

The services sector is the largest contributor to the Philippine economy, with business process outsourcing (BPO) being a major industry. Other key areas include tourism, retail, and financial services.

Manufacturing

The manufacturing sector includes electronics, textiles, food processing, and automotive industries. The Philippines is a major exporter of electronic products and garments.

OFWs & Remittances

Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) play a crucial role in the economy by sending remittances back home. These funds significantly contribute to household incomes and the national economy.
